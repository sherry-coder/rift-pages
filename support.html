Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data safe?

Yes. All your data is stored locally on your device. We never collect, transmit, or have access to any of your content.

How do I delete my data?

You can delete individual desired realities from the My DRs tab. To remove all app data, simply delete the app from your device.

Can I transfer my data to a new device?

Your Rift data is included in your standard iPhone backup. When you restore a backup to a new device, your Rift data will transfer automatically.

The app is not working properly.

Try closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, please contact us with a description of the problem and your device model.

I have a feature suggestion.

We love hearing from the shifting community! Please email us with your ideas and we'll consider them for future updates.
